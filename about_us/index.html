WHAT IS

FILMEPENTRUFILME

 ?
Films for Films is a non-profit campaign dedicated to supporting independent film productions and filmmakers, promoting storytelling, and transforming culture. We seek to discover, support, and inspire independent filmmakers from the United States and worldwide. We believe that stories allow individuals to share their authentic voice, entertain while pushing creative boundaries, and lead to social change.

Films for Films does not hold any specific social issue agenda. We support and encourage filmmakers to tell a full range of stories on film in whatever form fits the film and artist.

We host an auction with the collaboration and item donations of world-renowned actors, producers, and filmmakers. The funding created from this auction goes directly to support independent filmmakers and their projects. After concluding the auction, Films for Films fundraise resources and partners with charities to provide grants and support independent projects worldwide.

WHAT IS

FILMEPENTRUFILME
Films for Films is a non-profit campaign dedicated to supporting independent film productions and filmmakers, promoting storytelling, and transforming culture. We seek to discover, support, and inspire independent filmmakers from the United States and worldwide. We believe that stories allow individuals to share their authentic voice, entertain while pushing creative boundaries, and lead to social change.

Crowdfunding project

The Pitesti Experiment.

At the beginning of the 1950s, due to some circumstances, a student ends up as a political inmate.

This movie comes as a sincere and original approach of the controversial phenomenon inside Pitești Penitentiary, of which historian Francois Furet, member of the French Academy, believes to be “the most terrible inhuman experiences of our age”. His opinion is also supported by the 1970 Nobel Prize Winner in Literature, Alexandr Soljenitin, who defines Romanian Socialist Reformation as “the ultimate contemporary barbarism”.

“Herman” is a disturbing, yet fascinating story, born out in the unsettled times at the beginning of the regime, when the line between friends and foes was almost invisible. It is a terrifying account of self betrayal, faith, family and origin denial. Pretty much everything that lies at the core of human foundation

Our Founder

Victoria Baltag

Founder of filmepentrufilme


Victoria Baltag, founder and creator of the Projects, is a Romanian film director, screenwriter, and producer.

She has a degree in Sociology and Journalism from the University of Bucharest. In 2010, she completed a master's degree in Management at the Academy of Economic Studies in Bucharest and, in 2011, a master's degree in Film, History, and Television at the University of Birmingham.
